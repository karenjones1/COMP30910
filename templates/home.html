About the Wild Atlantic Way

The Wild Atlantic Way is a scenic coastal drive which spans accross 9 Western counties. Explore the views and extraordinary scenery along the route and dive into the culture.

The Outdoors

The Wild Atlantic Way offers spectacular scenery. You can take in the sights the Western counties have to offer from the beautiful beaches in Donegal to the lakes and mountains of Kilarney. The Wild Atlantic Way has something for everyone.

Golf

Enjoy golf accross the island of Ireland. Offering world-class golf courses in extraordinary locations, theres no better place to look than those on the Wild Alantic Way. With both links and parkland courses on offer, you won't know where to play first!

Food

Come and enjoy the food that Ireland has to offer. Situated along the coast, the Wild Atlantic Way is no short of shellfish fresh from the Atlantic. From Clonakilty Blackpudding, Mulroy Bay Mussels and Achill Mountain Lamb. The Wild Atlantic Way is full of diversity and flavour.
